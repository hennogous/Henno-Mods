## 1. Materials

* {{base}} Base Materials improvements: +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Water Mill or Wind Mill.
   * +2 {{production}} Production and +2 {{gold}} Gold to an Industry, and +3 {{production}} Production and +3 {{gold}} Gold to a Corporation.
* {{spec}} Specialty Materials improvements: +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Café.
   * +2 {{production}} Production and +2 {{gold}} Gold to an Industry, and +3 {{production}} Production and +3 {{gold}} Gold to a Corporation.

## 2. Water Mill or Wind Mill

The Water Mill has moved from the City Center to the Bakers' Quarter, and is only available if the Quarter has an adjacent river. The Wind Mill fills the same stage 2 role when the Quarter is not river-adjacent, and receives its terrain logistics bonus from Hills instead.

* Unlocks at Wheel
* Costs 80 {{production}} Production
* Maintenance: -2 {{gold}} Gold

* +1 {{production}} Production from every 2 adjacent river edges to the Water Mill, or +1 {{production}} Production from being built on Hills terrain to the Wind Mill.
* +1 {{food}} Food from each adjacent {{base}} Base Materials improvement.
   * +2 {{food}} Food from an Industry, and +3 {{food}} Food from a Corporation.
* +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold from the local Bakery and Café, providing +1 {{food}} Food to each in return.
* +1 {{food}} Food to an adjacent Granary, and +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold to the Water Mill or Wind Mill in return.
* At Feudalism, a Water Mill or Wind Mill adjacent to an improved {{base}} Base Materials resource unlocks:
   * A Storekeeper is established in the adjacent City Center if it has a Granary, and provides the city with +10% growth and +1 {{engineer}} Great Engineer point per turn.

## 3. Bakery

* Unlocks at Guilds
* Requires a Water Mill or Wind Mill in the Quarter
* Costs 160 {{production}} Production
* Maintenance: -2 {{gold}} Gold

* +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold to the local Water Mill or Wind Mill, in return for +1 {{food}} Food.
* +1 {{citizen}} Citizen slot, and +2 {{food}} Food to {{citizen}} Citizens in the Quarter.
* +1 {{amenity}} Amenity in the city.
* +0.1 {{food}} Food per {{citizen}} Citizen to each adjacent Market's city, and +0.1 {{production}} Production and +0.1 {{gold}} Gold per {{citizen}} Citizen to the Bakery's city in return.
* At Medieval Faires, a Bakery adjacent to an improved {{base}} Base Materials resource unlocks:
   * An Innkeeper is established in an adjacent Commercial Hub with a Market.
   * The Innkeeper provides the city with +2 {{housing}} Housing, +1 {{citizen}} Citizen slot and +1 {{merchant}} Great Merchant point per turn.
* +1 {{food}} Food bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold to the Quarter in return, if the origin city does not have a Bakers' Quarter. +1 {{amenity}} Amenity to the origin city.

## 4. Café

* Unlocks at The Enlightenment
* Requires a Water Mill or Wind Mill in the Quarter
* Costs 250 {{production}} Production
* Maintenance: -3 {{gold}} Gold

* +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold to the local Water Mill or Wind Mill, in return for +1 {{food}} Food.
* +1 {{food}} Food from each adjacent {{spec}} Specialty Materials improvement.
   * +2 {{food}} Food from an Industry, and +3 {{food}} Food from a Corporation.
* +1 {{citizen}} Citizen slot, and an additional +1 {{food}} Food and +1 {{culture}} Culture to {{citizen}} Citizens in the Quarter.
* +1 {{amenity}} Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles.
* +1 {{culture}} Culture for every 5 {{citizen}} Citizens in the city to each adjacent Zoo, Ferris Wheel or Conservatory (Leugi's Garden District), and +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold to the Café in return.
* At Urbanization, a Café adjacent to improved {{base}} Base and {{spec}} Specialty Materials resources unlocks:
   * A Groundskeeper is established in an adjacent Entertainment Complex with a Zoo, a Ride Technician in a Water Park with a Ferris Wheel, and a Horticulturist in a Garden district with a Conservatory (Leugi).
   * All three services provide the city with +2 {{tourism}} Tourism and +1 {{citizen}} Citizen slot.
   * The Groundskeeper and Ride Technician provide +1 {{engineer}} Great Engineer point per turn, while the Horticulturist provides +1 {{artist}} Great Artist point per turn.
* +1 {{food}} Food bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 {{production}} Production and +1 {{gold}} Gold to the Quarter in return, if the origin city does not have a Bakers' Quarter. +1 {{amenity}} Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles of the origin city.
