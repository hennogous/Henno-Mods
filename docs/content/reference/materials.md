# Materials Table

Many resources from the standard game (including Rise & Fall, Gathering Storm and the New Frontier Pass) have been mapped as either base materials or specialty materials for use in supply chains. Resources directly linked to animal exploitation are marked in *italics* and can optionally be included.

| Quarter | Base materials (Standard) | Base materials (Mods) | Specialty materials (Standard) | Specialty materials (Mods) |
|---------|--------------------------|----------------------|-------------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Bakers'** | Bananas, *Cattle*, Maize, Rice, Wheat | Barley (R2), Flax (CSC), Quinoa (LAR), Sorghum (R2) | Cocoa, Coffee, Grapes (Wine), Honey, Olives, Salt, Spices, Sugar, Tea | Berries (R2), Dates (R2), Poppies (R2), Saffron (R2), Strawberry (R2), Sugar Maple (R2), Tomato (R2) |
| **Tailors'** | *Cotton*, *Sheep* | Bamboo (R2), Flax (CSC), Hemp (CH), *Llamas* (LAR) | Dyes, Silk, Silver | Cashmere (R2), Gold (R2/SR) |
| **Apothecaries'** | Cocoa, Coffee, Honey, Incense, Olives, Spices, Tea, Tobacco | Algae (R2), Aloe (R2), Cannabis (CH), Coca (LAR), Coral (R2/SO), Hemp (CH), Kelp (SO), Medicinal Herbs (R2), Mushrooms (R2), Poppies (R2), Sandalwood (R2), Seashells (R2), Sponge (R2), Yerba Mate (LAR) | Copper, Iron, Mercury, Silver | Gold (R2/SR) |

## Compatible Resource Mods

- **CR** — CIVITAS Resources by Sailor Cat, thecrazyscotsman, SeelingCat, chimp and p0kiehl
- **LAR** — Latin American Resources by Leugi
- **R2** — Resourceful 2 by Amatheria, p0kiehl and Zegangani
- **SO** — Sukritact's Oceans by Sukritact
- **SR** — Sukritact's Resources by Sukritact
- **JNRW** — Wetlands by JNR
- **CH** — *(Cannabis/Hemp mod)*
- **CSC** — New resources introduced by this mod (e.g. Flax)
